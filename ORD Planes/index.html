ORD Planes

Objective:

The plane overhead has a problem with its luggage storage. It is dropping luggage all over the place. Your objective is to catch as much luggage as you can within three minutes. Be careful though, if you miss more than xxxx pieces of luggage, it will be game over!

How to play:

Touch the 'LEFT' and 'RIGHT' buttons to move the truck left and right. You will automatically catch luggage if it lands on your truck.
